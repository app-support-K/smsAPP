Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I add my alarm panel?

To add your alarm panel, navigate to the 'My Panels' section in the app and tap 'Add New Panel.' You'll need to enter the SIM card number associated with your alarm panel and configure the specific SMS commands for arming/disarming.

Why does the app open the Messages app?

Our app uses SMS commands to communicate with your alarm panel via its SIM card. When you tap 'Arm' or 'Disarm,' the app prepares an SMS message for you in your device's Messages app. You simply need to tap 'Send' to complete the command. This ensures your control and privacy.

My command isn't working. What should I do?

Please ensure your alarm panel's SIM card has active credit/plan and is receiving signal. Also, verify that the SIM number entered in the app is correct. If the issue persists, contact your alarm panel installer or refer to your panel's manual for specific SMS command syntax.

What alarm panels are supported?

The app is designed to work with most GSM/SMS-enabled alarm panels that accept specific SMS commands for arming, disarming, and other functions. You will need to know the specific commands for your panel to configure the app correctly.
